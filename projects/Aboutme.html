Hello, my name is Ammon Nulu

I was born and raised in Hyderabad, India. I grew up learning the gospel of Jesus Christ. My father is a returned missionary. He saw that in our village there was no English medium school. He talked with his missionary friends about it, and with God help they started teaching children. I studied in that school until 5th class. After that, I went to another school and studied there until 10th class. Later, I joined college and finished 12th class. For 10th, 11th, and 12th, I paid my own school fees. I used to work in the daytime and study in the evenings. At the end of the year, I wrote my exams.

After that, I worked for a few years and went on a mission. When I finished my mission, I applied to BYU-Hawaii and got admission. I came to BYU-Hawaii for my undergraduate studies. As a bonus, I can work part-time and I also got an opportunity to work in the temple. Here in Hawaii I like some foods.

  4. Malasada
