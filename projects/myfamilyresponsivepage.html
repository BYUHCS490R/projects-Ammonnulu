Fun Facts About the Doraemon Family

  • Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century.
  • Nobita is kind hearted but often lazy and unlucky.
  • Doraemon helps Nobita with futuristic gadgets from his pocket.
  • Shizuka, Gian, and Suneo are Nobita's close friends.

Nobisuke is Nobita's dad. He works hard every day in the office to take care of his family. He likes to read the newspaper and watch TV after work. Nobisuke is friendly and patient, and he often tells Nobita to keep trying and never give up.

Tamako is Nobita's mom. She cooks, cleans, and takes care of the whole family. She sometimes shouts at Nobita when he is lazy, but she truly loves him. Tamako always wants Nobita to study well and grow up to be a good and kind person.

Nobita is a sweet and funny boy who likes to play more than study. He often gets into trouble because he forgets to do his homework. Even when things go wrong, Nobita has a good heart and always tries to help his friends. With Doraemon's help, he learns to be brave and do better each day.
