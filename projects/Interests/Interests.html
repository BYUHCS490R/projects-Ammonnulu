Biryani: My Favorite Food

Beef Biryani

AlluArjun Cooking

Kings food

My favorite food to cook and eat is Beef Biryani. Because it was a royal food, in ancient times,kings used to eat it. Over the generations, the cooking skills were passed down so that we can still enjoy this yummy dish today.

I first saw biryani in a restaurant. Later, I wanted to cook it myself for family and friends. My Muslim friends taught me their recipe. Now I know how to cook it, and I feel happy when I make it at home. It is more than food for me, it is also about culture and friendship.
