South Indian Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish with meat or vegetables, cooked with spices and herbs. South India has different varieties:

  • Hyderabadi Biryani - rich, spicy, often served with yogurt
  • Kerala Biryani - uses coconut and local spices
  • Andhra Biryani - very spicy and flavorful
  • Tamil Nadu Biryani - lighter spices, fragrant rice
