Chai

Chai (tea) is a daily staple in South India. It is made by boiling tea leaves with milk, water, and spices like cardamom and ginger. Chai is often enjoyed with snacks in the evening.

Filter Coffee

South Indian filter coffee is a strong, aromatic coffee served with milk and sugar. It is an important part of daily life and cultural gatherings.
