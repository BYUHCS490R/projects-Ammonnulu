South Indian Spices

South Indian food uses many spices to enhance aroma and flavor. Common spices include:

Spice Flavor Dishes Used In Health Benefits
Cardamom Sweet, aromatic Chai, desserts, biryani Aids digestion
Cumin Earthy, warm Sambar, curries Helps digestion
Mustard Seeds Nutty, pungent Sambar, pickles Anti-inflammatory
Turmeric Warm, bitter Curries, biryani Antioxidant
