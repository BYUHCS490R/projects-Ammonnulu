Dosa

Dosa is a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Popular varieties include masala dosa, rava dosa, and paper dosa.

Idli

Idli is a soft, steamed cake made from fermented batter. It is light and healthy, usually served with chutney and sambar.
