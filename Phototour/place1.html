Place 2 - Mormon Tebernacle Choir

The Mormon Tabernacle Choir is a large singing group from Salt Lake City, Utah. It is part of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and has singers who volunteer their time. The choir is famous around the world for its beautiful music and weekly program called *Music and the Spoken Word.
