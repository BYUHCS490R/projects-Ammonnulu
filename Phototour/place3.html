Place 3 - Jordan River Temple

This Temple is one of the temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It is located in South Jordan, Utah, near the Jordan River. The temple is a peaceful and beautiful place where members go to worship and make sacred promises with God.
