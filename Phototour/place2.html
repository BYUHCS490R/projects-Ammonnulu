Place 2 - Restoration of the Melchizedek Priesthood statue

Near Temple Square, there is a statue of Peter, James, and John giving the Melchizedek Priesthood to Joseph Smith and Oliver Cowdery. This is an important event in the history of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The statue reminds everyone about the restoration of priesthood power in the church.
