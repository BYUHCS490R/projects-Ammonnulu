What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags always start on a new line and take the full width of the page. Inline tags stay on the same line and take only the space they need.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is space outside the element.Border is line around the element. Padding is space inside the element, around the text or picture.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Fixed is always same size. Flexible is change with screen

When would you use a figure tag?

Use the figure tag for pictures, charts, or videos that have a caption
