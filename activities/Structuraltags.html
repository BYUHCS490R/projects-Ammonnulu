What goes in the header?

The header has the title, logo, or top part of the website. It can also have a menu or search bar.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

The nav has the main links for the website, like Home, About, or Contact. It can be inside the header, footer, or anywhere, and it may link to other sites too.

What goes in Main?

The main has the main content of the page, like articles, text, images, or videos. It should be only one per page.

What goes in the Footer?

The footer has the bottom part of the website with copyright, contact info, or extra links. It can also have social media or site maps.

Structural Tags

header, nav, main, footer
